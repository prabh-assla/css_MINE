DEAD SOULS!
as i shout
sad lyrics
those
onto the nakedness
of those cold walled rooms,
the memories of those
who deceased
or left me
keep on dehydrating my lips,
as i starve
of kisses and caressing hands;
starve of love and hope in general;

my laughter diminishing into
silence which is being
raped by irregular heart beats
of my decaying heart;
"while"
they keep on smiling
looking how joyous
i have become
without them;
but little did they know,
the yellow joy they see
is nothing worth to me;

"while"
living their happiness
in a static phase,
a constant depressing phase;
as they remain stuck
to all those
paint-less ugly old walls,
walls that
sheltered artifacts;
sheltered floors —
covered with
now burnt oxygen that once travelled the
viens of all those depressed souls who died in that room...

those ROUGH walls —
weakened
hopeless
strength-less
stare at me...
while i
attempt to relock the door
once again
to come back again
after zillion of years...
- pd singh
