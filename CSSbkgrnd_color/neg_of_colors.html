On 15th of August, 1947, India became an independent country. At this time, the country didn't have a constitution of its own. On 28th of August, 1947, a drafting committee with Dr. B. R. Ambedkar as the head had been appointed to draft a permanent constitution for the country.
Subsequently the drafting committee submitted its first report on 4th of Nov, 1947. Followed by discussions and revisions, the report was then signed by the Constituent Assembly on 24th of Jan, 1950.
Two days later, on 26th of Jan, 1950, the constitution came into effect. Now, India stands as a truly sovereign country with a constitution of its own.
Designed by the inventor of the radio, “Marconi” and managed by “The Wireless and Telegraph Company” which was owned by Marconi himself, radio signaling system of the Titanic must had been one of the most powerful of that time. Adding to features of radio signaling, all messages were sent wirelessly via Morse Code and could be accessed by anybody on that particular frequency.
Even after having one of the most powerful radio signaling system, the reason Titanic collided with iceberg may be that the operators on the Titanic were busy handling the communication of high- profile passengers on board and hence could not receive warnings about the iceberg.
Another reason may be that many ships received the distress call from the Titanic, but the messages were being transmitted and retransmitted, leading to a confusion in the messages. Sadly when the disaster struck, help couldn't reach the Titanic on time. The ship sank and many lives were lost.
